Three Ingredient Peanut Butter Cookies.

Description

This recipe has just 3 ingredients. It's fast, easy, and the cookies are wonderful! It's great for kids that are just learning to bake.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.

  2. Step 2

    3. Combine the peanut butter, white sugar and egg. Mix until smooth.

  3. Step 3

    4. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the prepared baking sheet. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 6 to 8 minutes. Do not overbake! These cookies are best when they are still soft and just barely brown on the bottoms.
